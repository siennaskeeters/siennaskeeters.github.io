# Fall 2026 10U Baseball Tryouts

The Sienna Skeeters are getting ready for Fall 2026. Baseball players interested in the 10U program are invited to attend tryouts on **Sunday, July 26**, at **Sienna Field 5**.

## Tryout details

- **Date:** Sunday, July 26, 2026
- **Tryout time:** 3–5 p.m.
- **Check-in:** 2:30 p.m.
- **Location:** Sienna Field 5
- **Bring:** Baseball gear and water

Please plan to arrive in time for check-in. For RSVP information and additional details, visit [siennaskeeters.com](https://www.siennaskeeters.com).
